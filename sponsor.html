Why Sponsor?

We connect you to some of the next generation of IT thinkers and entrepreneurs.

Sponsoring UNIHACK is a great way to connect with the top IT students from the University of Melbourne, Monash University, and other universities across Australia. Similar hackathons in the United States have provided sponsors access to many talented students who end up working for them in a couple of years.

As a sponsor, you get to:

  • Meet and connect with the best students from Australia, and get early access to them before any other company;
  • Showcase your products and APIs, and see talented students make something awesome with them;
  • Mentor, inspire and encourage students with their idea and how to accomplish it within 24 hours
